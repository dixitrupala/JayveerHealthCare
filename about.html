Who WE Are

Jayveer Healthcare

“ Established in the year 1991, we 'Jayveer Healthcare' are one of the reliable and pre-eminent organizations of this domain, actively engaged in Manufacturing and Exporting a quality guaranteed accumulation of Veterinary Medicinal Products. We have attained ISO 9001:2008 certification through products offered by us such as Multivitamin Injections, Ceftriaxone Injection, and Toldimphos Injection. Produced medicines are publicly acknowledged and appreciated across the market for their efficient performance, longer shelf life, and excellent formulation. Our given medicines are reviewed before the ultimate dispatch to the clients under the guidance of skillful and highly trained quality controllers. Presently, we have a broad spectrum of therapeutic and nutritional medicines for both large and small animals in the national and abroad markets.

We have installed a rich infrastructure system in the market to offer an ample scope of veterinary medicinal medications. Owing to the precise formation ability of our advanced manufacturing unit, we have been capable of fulfilling the bulk order requirements of our esteemed customers in a convenient manner. Furthermore, faithful efforts and dedicated assistance of our extremely proficient and experienced workforce encouraged us to expand our range across the market. For the convenient of our precious customers, we accept different modes of payment such as Online Fund Transfer, Cheque, Cash, and DD. Owing to these prospects, we have attained vast clientele base from countries such as Nepal, Bangladesh, Myanmar, Sri Lanka and Afghanistan. ”
